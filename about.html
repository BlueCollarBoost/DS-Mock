Meet the Owner

MS

Mike S. is the founder and Master Electrician behind DS Electric. Starting out as an apprentice right here in Windsor, Mike worked his way up from residential service calls to complex commercial and industrial projects across Essex County.

With over a decade in the field, Mike’s philosophy is simple: do the job right, treat every client with respect, and never cut corners. He’s personally overseen everything from new home builds and major panel upgrades to troubleshooting tough issues that other contractors walk away from.

Mike’s team is small by design—every project gets his hands-on attention and the highest level of care. He believes in:

  • Clear, honest communication from quote to finish
  • Showing up on time and doing what we say we’ll do
  • Staying up-to-date with code and new technology
  • Building lasting relationships with customers

In his free time, you’ll find Mike helping out at community events, taking courses to keep his skills sharp, or volunteering to wire up local non-profits.
